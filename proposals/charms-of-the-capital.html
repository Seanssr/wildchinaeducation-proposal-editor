Charms of the Capital

Immerse yourself in Beijing's living history as you explore hidden hutongs, hike the Great Wall, and connect with locals through martial arts, community service, and hands-on cultural experiences that bring the capital's rich heritage to life.

Program Overview

Experience the Heart of Beijing

Experience the heart of Beijing through interactive adventures — from racing through ancient hutongs and exploring the Forbidden City to practicing calligraphy, Kung Fu, and crafting your own Peking Opera mask.

Hike the Great Wall at Mutianyu, savor iconic local dishes like Peking duck and mutton hotpot, and connect with locals through dumpling-making, square dancing, and community surveys.

Wrap up your journey by volunteering at a local orphanage, leaving with unforgettable memories and a deeper understanding of China's culture, history, and people.
